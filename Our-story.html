LOIS & SAMUEL

How we met

Lois and Samuel went to the same secondary school, but they were from completely different worlds. Lois was the quiet, studious girl who always had her nose buried in a book, while Samuel was the class clown, always surrounded by friends and cracking jokes. They knew each other, of course—everyone knew Samuel—but their paths never really crossed. Lois stayed focused on her academics, and Samuel spent his time making people laugh and occasionally getting into harmless trouble with the teachers.

Fast forward three years after graduation, Lois was out running errands at the local mall. She was standing in line at a café when she heard someone say, "Lois? Is that you?" She turned around and, to her surprise, saw Samuel standing there, grinning from ear to ear. He hadn’t changed much—same bright smile, same easygoing vibe.

"Samuel? Wow, it’s been ages!" Lois said, genuinely surprised.

"Yeah, it has! I almost didn’t recognize you without all those textbooks in your arms," Samuel joked, causing Lois to roll her eyes.

They ended up sitting down for coffee, catching up on where life had taken them since school. Samuel told her about his adventures trying to figure out what he wanted to do with his life, while Lois shared how she’d been busy with university. They laughed over old school memories—like the time Samuel got in trouble for making the entire class laugh during a serious test—and discovered they had more in common than they thought.

What started as a simple catch-up turned into regular hangouts, and soon they found themselves looking forward to each other’s company more than anything else. Samuel's jokes now made Lois laugh, and Lois’s calm nature had a way of balancing out Samuel’s playful spirit.

And, as they say, the rest is history.
